Puzzle Promenade Chain Puzzle Generating Website Conjunction Junction Puzzle Conjunction Junction Puzzles are word puzzles in which you are given all of the letters of the first word or phrase except for the first letter and then you need to use a conjunction (AND, BUT, and OR) to connect it to a second word or clause, which is also missing its first letter, that is given in the word bank beneath the main puzzle.
